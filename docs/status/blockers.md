## Blocker Template

When adding a blocker, use this format:

```markdown
### [BLOCKER-001] Title

**Created**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Owner**: Who is responsible for resolving
**Status**: Active | Investigating | Resolved

**Description**:
What is blocked and why.

**Impact**:
What work is affected by this blocker.

**Resolution Path**:
Steps being taken to resolve.

**Updates**:
- YYYY-MM-DD: Update text
```
